Abies spectabilis (D. Don) Spach.

Abies spectabilis (D. Don) Spach.

Common Name: Talish patra, Gobra salla (Nepal

Family: PINACEAE

Medicinal Properties:

Respiratory aid, Skin treatment, Pain relief.

Cultivation

Climate:

Cool, mountainous regions with ample rainfall are ideal for its cultivation.

Soil: Prefers deep, fertile, and well-drained soil with a slightly acidic pH.

Planting: Propagated through seeds; grown in nurseries and transplanted after a few years.

Watering: Regular watering during dry seasons is essential, especially for young trees.

Fertilizing: A balanced fertilizer every few years is recommended.

Harvesting

Resin:

Tap the resin by making incisions in the bark, collecting it in containers below the cuts.

Needles:

Harvest needles by pruning or collecting from the ground.

Note: Sustainable harvesting practices are essential to protect the tree population.
